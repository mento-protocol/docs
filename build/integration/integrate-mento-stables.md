# Deployments & addresses

To integrate Mento stables in your app or exchange you need contract addresses and deployment details for the chains where Mento V3 is live.

* **Addresses:** [Deployments → Addresses](../deployments/addresses.md) — Stable token and FPMM contract addresses per network.
* **Deployments:** [Deployments](../deployments/README.md) — Parameters, verification, and deployment overview.

For Celo integration in general (wallets, RPC, etc.), see [docs.celo.org](https://docs.celo.org/integration).

**FiatConnect** provides a standardized API for Web3 wallets and dApps to integrate multiple on/off ramps. See [fiatconnect.org](https://fiatconnect.org/).

If you are **listing Mento Stables** on your exchange, you may need Mento Platform brand assets; contact the team or see the [Mento frontend monorepo](https://github.com/mento-protocol/frontend-monorepo) for token icons and branding.
