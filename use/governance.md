# Governance

Mento: token holders and community. Participate, tokenomics, components, watchdogs.

---

## Participating

- **Stake:** MENTO → e.g. veMENTO for voting power.
- **Vote:** Proposals (parameters, upgrades, treasury) via governance UI.
- **Propose:** Eligible participants; follow process and templates.

---

## Tokenomics

- **MENTO:** Governance and utility. Voting, proposals, incentives (LP, rebalance), other roles per governance.
- **veMENTO:** Locked MENTO for higher voting power; duration may affect weight. See governance docs.

---

## Components

- **Governance contract(s):** Execute approved proposals. Build docs for addresses.
- **Treasury:** Protocol-owned; governance-controlled.
- **Parameters:** Fee, rebalance incentive, trading limits, etc. updatable via governance.

---

## Watchdogs and safety

- **Watchdogs:** Monitor state (oracle, limits, insolvency); can pause or escalate.
- **Pause / breakers:** Governance or watchdogs can pause trading or rebalancing. [Limits & breakers](../concepts/limits-and-breakers.md), [Security](security.md).
