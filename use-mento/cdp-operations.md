# CDP operations (borrow, repay, stability pool)

*This page is a placeholder. Full CDP (collateralized debt position) docs for Mento v3 will be added here.*

On Mento v3, some stablecoins (e.g. **GBPm**) are **synthetic**: they are created when users **borrow** against collateral, similar to Liquity-style CDPs. You can:

- **Borrow** — Deposit collateral and mint the stablecoin (e.g. GBPm).
- **Repay** — Return the stablecoin to close (or reduce) your debt and free collateral.
- **Stability pool** — Deposit the stablecoin into a stability pool that is used when liquidations occur; you may earn rewards.

In practice, use the **[Mento app](https://app.mento.org/)** for borrow, repay, and stability pool flows. Contract and integration details will be documented in the Build section.
