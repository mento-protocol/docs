## v3 highlights

- **FPMMs:** Swap at oracle rate (minus fee). No curve slippage; value per share at oracle.
- **Rebalancing:** Allowlisted strategies (Reserve, CDP) rebalance when composition drifts. Capped incentive; value protection and trading limits.
- **CDP stables:** GBPm (and future) use Liquity-style CDP: borrow, repay, stability pool.
- **Oracle and breakers:** Recency, trading mode, FX hours gate trading. Trading limits cap netflow per token.

Full mechanics: [How it works & stablecoins](intro.md), [Concepts](../concepts/fpmm.md).
